Block-level Elements

A block-level element always starts on a new line and takes up the full width available (stretches out to the left and right as far as it can).

  1. <address>
  2. <article>
  3. <aside>
  4. <blockquote>
  5. <canvas>
  6. <dd>
  7. <div>
  8. <dl>
  9. <dt>
  10. <fieldset>
  11. <figcaption>
  12. <figure>
  13. <footer>
  14. <form>
  15. <h1> - <h6>
  16. <header>
  17. <hr>
  18. <li>
  19. <main>
  20. <nav>
  21. <noscript>
  22. <ol>
  23. <p>
  24. <pre>
  25. <section>
  26. <table>
  27. <tfoot>
  28. <ul>
  29. <video>
